About

Thirty years navigating
every layer of the capital structure.

Aaron Welsh brings three decades of experience throughout the entire capital structure, with a deep command of capital and property markets across all asset classes. His track record spans origination, structuring, restructuring, and principal investment at the highest levels of institutional real estate finance.

Most recently, Aaron was a Partner at Apollo Global Management in its real estate credit group. He served as Managing Director and Head of Origination, and Co-Head of Bank of America's Real Estate Structured Finance business, where he also led the investment bank's global real estate restructurings. Aaron was a Managing Director at Ladder Capital and previously held senior roles at Barclays Capital, Lehman Brothers, and JLL (Jones Lang Wootton).

Aaron is a vocal mental health advocate, volunteering with and supporting organizations committed to ending the stigma of mental illness.
